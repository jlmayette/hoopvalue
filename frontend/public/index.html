Orphans to recruit? Dispersal to fill? Sorted.

Never get bogged down in a massive spreadsheet ever again. Highlight orphans or dispersal teams in the Power Rankings in minutes — just add a quick label to the team names that need filling, copy the URL, and get recruiting.

For orphans, add any of Open, New Manager, or Orphan in parentheses or brackets in the team's name — that team gets highlighted with a red number. For dispersal, any parens or brackets containing Dispersal get a gray number, and all assets get collected in one place below.
